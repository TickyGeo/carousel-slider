Carousel slider

Preview ![preview](preview.png)

This is a carousel slider, in which you can change the slides by the wheel of your mouse or buttons. You even can do it with dragging.

I used HTML, CSS and JS for this project.  When you drag the slides or spin the mouse wheel or push on of the buttons your sliders spine around the axis. On 45 degrees. And the sign of degree depends on direction of spinning or dragging or posittion of the button.
